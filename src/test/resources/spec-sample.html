Good Android phone with 2.7GHz Quad-core processor provides good performance and great system execution of the Motorola Nexus 6 (XT1100 32GB).

With one SIM card slot, the Motorola Nexus 6 (XT1100 32GB) allows download up to 150 Mbps for internet browsing, but it also depends on the carrier.

Great connectivity of this device includes Bluetooth 4.0 + A2DP, WiFi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac [wifi5] (2.4GHz, 5GHz) and NFC to make mobile payments or connection to other devices.

Device with 184 grams including the battery. The Motorola Nexus 6 (XT1100 32GB) phone is relatively thin with 10.1mm thickness.

4.7/10
